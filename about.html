Our Story

Bringing the Best Tech to Narok 🇰🇪

UrbanTech started in 2019 as a small shop in Maasai Mara. We were frustrated by overpriced, unwarranted electronics flooding Narok's markets. Our mission was simple — sell genuine, affordable tech with real warranty support.

Today, we serve over 10,000 happy customers across Kenya with same-day delivery in Narok, and we're just getting started.
