📦 Orders & Delivery

How long does delivery take in Narok?+
We offer same-day delivery within Narok Maasai Mara for orders placed before 2:00 PM. For other areas within Narok, expect delivery within 1–2 business days. Outside Narok, standard delivery takes 2–4 business days.
How do I track my order?+
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive an SMS and WhatsApp message with a tracking link. You can also log in to your account dashboard and click "Track Order" on any order.
Is there free delivery?+
Yes! All orders above KSh 5,000 qualify for free delivery within Narok. For orders below this amount, a flat delivery fee of KSh 300 applies. We also offer free in-store pickup from our Maasai Mara branch anytime during business hours.
Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?+
You can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting us on WhatsApp (+254 745 663044). Once the order has been dispatched, we are unable to cancel it, but you can use our 30-day return policy.

💳 Payment

What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept M-Pesa (Paybill and Till), Visa/Mastercard debit & credit cards, bank transfer, and cash on delivery within Narok. M-Pesa is the fastest and most popular option — you'll get an STK push directly to your phone.
Is it safe to pay online on UrbanTech?+
Absolutely. Our website uses SSL encryption (HTTPS) to protect all transactions. Card payments are processed via a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We never store your card details on our servers.
Do you offer installment / buy-now-pay-later?+
Yes! We partner with M-Pesa Fanikiwa and select banks to offer 3–12 month installment plans on purchases above KSh 20,000. Contact us on WhatsApp to set this up before placing your order.

🛡️ Products & Warranty

Are all your products genuine / original?+
100%. Every product we sell is sourced directly from authorized distributors or official brand partners. Apple products come with Kenya Apple warranty, Samsung with Samsung Kenya warranty, and so on.
What is the warranty period?+
All devices come with a manufacturer's warranty of at least 12 months from the date of purchase. Accessories are covered for 6 months. UrbanTech also provides an additional 3-month warranty on top of the manufacturer's warranty for selected products.
How do I claim warranty?+
Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your order number, a description of the issue, and photos/video of the defect. We will guide you through the process. Most warranty claims are resolved within 3–7 business days.

🔄 Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?+
We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original, unused condition with all accessories and packaging intact. Defective items are returned at no cost to you. Change-of-mind returns may attract a small restocking fee.
How long do refunds take?+
M-Pesa refunds are processed within 24 hours once the return is approved. Bank transfers take 2–3 business days. Card refunds may take 5–10 business days depending on your bank. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

👤 Account

Do I need an account to order?+
No — you can checkout as a guest. However, creating a free account lets you track orders, save your wishlist, view order history, and checkout faster next time.
I forgot my password. What should I do?+
On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam/junk folder.
